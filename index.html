Luftblasen

Luftblasen is a multi-themed math-based puzzle game. Move fast and think faster to pop bubbles and add them up to the required total (just don't go over) to score points and move to the next round.

Quiz Your Lizard

Quiz Your lizard for iPhone and iPad lets you create your own study material, and view it how you like; on the go, and in one of three modes - Study, Pop Quiz, and Spelling. You can even share your creations with others. Several samples are included, but the true power is creating your own quizzes, whether it be for a class, learning a foreign language, or for fun. You can also enable reverse mode; you will be prompted with the answer, and you provide the question.
